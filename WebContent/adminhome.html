Focus On Healthy Environment

About Us

our mission is to revolutionize food packaging by providing a sustainable, innovative alternative to bisphenol A (BPA). We are dedicated to harnessing the power of tomato pomace a byproduct of tomato processing as a cutting-edge solution for creating BPA free coatings. Our commitment is to develop eco-friendly products that not only address health concerns associated with BPA but also contribute to reducing food packaging waste. By integrating advanced technology and sustainable practices, we aim to deliver high-quality, environmentally responsible solutions that set new standards in the food packaging industry.
