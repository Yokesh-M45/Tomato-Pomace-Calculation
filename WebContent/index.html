Advanced Food Can Coatings

Tomato pomace is a byproduct of the tomato processing industry, making it a readily available and renewable resource.

About

The project aims to develop an innovative food can coating using tomato pomace, a byproduct of tomato processing, to replace bisphenol A (BPA)-based coatings. The goal is to create a safer, environmentally friendly alternative that maintains the protective qualities of traditional coatings while addressing health and sustainability concerns.
