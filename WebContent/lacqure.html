Use Of Ethuyl Alcohol

  • Ethyl alcohol, also known as ethanol, has a variety of uses across different industries and applications due to its chemical properties.

  • Ethyl alcohol is used as a solvent to extract vegetable fat from the tomato pomace. The combination of about 20% ethanol with 80% water helps dissolve and separate the desired components from the tomato waste.

  • the ethyl alcohol helps create a dispersion of the tomato-derived grease in water.

  • The ethanol-water mixture facilitates the resins ability to adhere firmly to the metal can. The resin formed with the help of ethanol ensures that it sticks well to the can and provides effective protection.

  • Ethanol contributes to the hydrophobic properties of the resin, making it resistant to water and other liquids. This enhances the protective capabilities of the coating, preventing corrosion and contamination of the cans contents.
