Repurposing Tomato Byproducts into a Protective Metal Coating

Tomato pomace, including skins, seeds, and stalks, is repurposed into a protective lacquer. This byproduct from tomato processing is typically discarded but is used here to create an environmentally friendly coating.

About Us

In response to health concerns associated with bisphenol A (BPA) in food packaging, a novel, eco-friendly alternative has been developed using tomato pomace, a byproduct of tomato processing. This study details the conversion of tomato pomace comprising tomato skins, seeds, and stalks into a bio based lacquer for metal food containers.
